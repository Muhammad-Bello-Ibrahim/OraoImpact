Who We Are

\n

Orao Impact Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to using technology and innovation to solve critical challenges in education, healthcare, and economic inclusion. We work at the intersection of digital transformation and social impact, empowering underserved communities with the tools, knowledge, and systems they need to thrive.
