About Orao Impact Foundation

Orao Impact Foundation is a technology-driven nonprofit building pathways to dignity and prosperity. We equip youth, entrepreneurs, and underserved communities with practical digital tools to unlock opportunity.

Digital Skills & Education

Equipping youth with in-demand digital competencies for the future of work.

Community Safety (meSafe)

Applying community-led safety technology to strengthen trust and resilience.

SME Digital Empowerment (Shopmon)

Helping small businesses thrive with digital visibility, tools, and market access.
