## The SME Opportunity

Small and medium enterprises (SMEs) account for over **80% of employment** across Africa and are the primary engine of economic growth in most countries on the continent. Yet the majority operate entirely offline — invisible to the growing base of digital consumers and cut off from digital tools that could transform their efficiency and reach.

**Shopmon** is Orao Impact Foundation's response to this challenge.

## What Is Shopmon?

Shopmon is the flagship initiative of our **SME Digital Growth Program** — a structured digital enablement journey for small business owners. Through Shopmon, SMEs gain access to:

- **Online presence setup** — a professional digital storefront that can be found on search engines and social media.
- **Digital marketing training** — practical skills in social media marketing, WhatsApp Business, and online advertising.
- **E-commerce tools** — guidance on selling online through existing platforms and building their own storefronts.
- **Financial tools introduction** — mobile payments, digital invoicing, and basic accounting software.
- **Peer learning network** — connecting SME owners for shared learning and referrals.

## Why SMEs Struggle to Go Digital

Despite the clear benefits of digitisation, many small business owners face significant barriers:

- **Cost concerns** — digital tools are perceived as expensive.
- **Skills gap** — owners lack confidence with technology.
- **Trust deficit** — scepticism about online payments and digital business models.
- **Time constraints** — running a small business leaves little room for learning new systems.
- **Limited support ecosystems** — few affordable advisory services exist.

Shopmon addresses each of these barriers through community-based training, peer mentorship, and subsidised access to tools.

## A Holistic Approach

> "We don't just teach SMEs how to use a tool. We walk with them through the entire journey — from mindset to market."

Our programme spans **8 weeks** of structured learning, combining:

- Group workshops (hands-on, practical, in local language)
- One-on-one coaching sessions
- Access to a digital toolkit (curated, vetted, affordable)
- A graduation showcase connecting SMEs with potential customers and investors

## Who Can Join?

The Shopmon programme is open to:

- Registered SME owners in our target communities.
- Informal traders looking to formalise and grow.
- Youth entrepreneurs launching their first businesses.

No prior digital knowledge is required — just the drive to grow.
