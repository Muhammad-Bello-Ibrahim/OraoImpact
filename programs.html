Programs Driving Real Community Impact

Three flagship programs, one shared purpose: empowering underserved communities across Africa through technology, safety, and economic opportunity.

Digital Skills Coding AI Basics Youth

Orao Digital Empowerment Program

A hands-on, community-based digital training initiative that equips young people with the skills they need to participate fully in today's digital economy — from coding fundamentals to AI literacy and online entrepreneurship.

🎯Focus: Digital literacy, coding fundamentals, AI basics
👥Target: Youth and students aged 16–30
⏱️Duration: 8–12 weeks per cohort
📍Mode: In-person community centres + online

Community Safety Reporting Civic Tech

Safe Communities Initiative (meSafe)

A community-driven safety platform that empowers residents to report incidents anonymously, enables rapid community response, and builds safer, more resilient neighbourhoods through technology and transparency.

🎯Focus: Community safety reporting and response systems
👥Target: Community members, local leaders, safety responders
📱Platform: Web, SMS, and low-bandwidth mobile
🔒Privacy: Anonymous reporting with end-to-end feedback
Join the Initiative Partner With Us
📝

Anonymous Reporting

Report safety concerns without fear of reprisal. Multiple reporting channels including SMS for low-connectivity areas.

⚡

Rapid Response

Verified community responders receive and act on reports quickly, with full accountability built into the workflow.

📊

Safety Analytics

Community leaders access aggregated safety data and trend reports to inform planning and resource allocation.

SME E-commerce Digital Marketing Shopmon

SME Digital Growth Program (Shopmon)

A structured 8-week digital enablement programme helping small and medium enterprises go online, market effectively, accept digital payments, and grow their customer base in the digital economy.

🎯Focus: Online presence, digital marketing, e-commerce, fintech tools
👥Target: SME owners, informal traders, youth entrepreneurs
⏱️Duration: 8 weeks structured programme
🎓Outcome: Graduation showcase connecting SMEs with buyers & investors
Apply for Shopmon Sponsor an SME
🌐

Digital Storefront

Get a professional online presence that is findable on Google, shareable on social media, and easy for customers to use.

📣

Digital Marketing

Practical training in social media marketing, WhatsApp Business, and targeted online advertising on a small business budget.

💳

Financial Tools

Mobile payments, digital invoicing, and simple accounting software to manage finances and build credibility with customers.
